Our History

A Journey of Faith Since 1991

Sikkim Bible College holds the historic distinction of being the first Government-registered Bible College in the state of Sikkim. Established in the year 2023, the college stands as a milestone in theological education within the region. It was founded with a clear vision to provide sound biblical training, academic excellence, and Christ-centered leadership development for the Church and society.

"We exist to equip the next generation of Christ-centered leaders for the Harvest in the Himalayas and beyond."
A Message from our President

Empowering Leaders, Transforming Society

I am Rev. Joseph Tamang, the president of Sikkim Bible College and I welcome you all to our ministries. We have launched Sikkim Bible College to train our Christian leaders. Simultaneously, we offer vocational courses like Diploma in Computer and English Spoken Course for each student, equipping them to serve society.

We are also supporting dropout students by providing tuition and assisting them in enrolling in the National Open School. In addition, we have launched the Sikkim Theological Journal to offer a platform for Indigenous authors. This journal fosters philosophical and theological debates through written discourse.

We kindly ask for your prayers for our ministries and encourage you to send students to our college.

May God bless you!

Rev. Joseph Tamang
