Our History

A Journey of Faith Since 1991

Sikkim Bible College holds the historic distinction of being the first Government-registered Bible College in the state of Sikkim. Established in the year 2026, the college stands as a milestone in theological education within the region. It was founded with a clear vision to provide sound biblical training, academic excellence, and Christ-centered leadership development for the Church and society.

"We exist to equip the next generation of Christ-centered leaders for the Harvest in the Himalayas and beyond."

Our Core Values

We hold the 66 books of the Bible as the final authority in all matters of faith and conduct. Every course we teach is filtered through the lens of God's Word.
Recognizing the diversity of the Himalayas, we train students to respect local traditions while presenting the unchanging Gospel with wisdom and grace.
We don't just train "bosses"; we train servants. Our model of leadership is Christ washing the feet of His disciples.
