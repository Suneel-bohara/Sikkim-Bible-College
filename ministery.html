Our Spiritual Pillars

At SBC, we believe that academic excellence must be balanced with a vibrant spiritual life and practical service.

⛪

Daily Chapel

Every morning, our community gathers for worship, prayer, and the Word. It is the heartbeat of our campus life where students and faculty seek God together.

🤝

Mentoring Groups

Students are placed in small "Timothy Groups" led by a faculty mentor. These groups provide a safe space for accountability, growth, and personal prayer.

🏔️

Weekend Ministry

Sikkim is our mission field. Every weekend, students visit local churches, lead youth programs, and conduct village outreaches across the East Sikkim region.

The Growth Cycle

Impact Beyond the Classroom

Our students don't just stay in Gangtok. Every year, during the winter break, we send mission teams to the remote "corners" of the Himalayas.

1

North Sikkim: Reaching high-altitude villages with the message of hope.

2

Border Missions: Serving communities along the Nepal and Bhutan borders.

3

Urban Youth: Partnering with Gangtok city churches for mega-youth rallies.
