Enrollment for 2026 Academic Year

General Requirements

We seek students who are spiritually vibrant, academically capable, and called to serve the body of Christ. Below are the basic eligibility criteria:

  • Minimum age of 18 years
  • Born-again Christian experience
  • Active member of a local church
  • Class 12 Pass (for B.Th)
  • B.Th or Graduate (for M.Div)
  • Proficiency in English

Application Process

Step 1: Download & Fill

Download the application packet and fill out all details manually or digitally.

Step 2: Recommendation

Obtain a recommendation letter from your local Church Pastor or Elder.

Step 3: Submission

Submit the forms along with previous academic transcripts and the application fee.
